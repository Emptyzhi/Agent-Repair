A short history of model theory Wilfrid Hodges Link This article provides a concise historical overview of model theory, tracing its development from its origins to the early 21st century. It is written in an accessible style and emphasizes the connections between model theory and other areas of mathematics.
A Historical Survey of the Model Theoretic Universe John T. Baldwin Link This survey provides a historical perspective on the development of model theory, with a focus on the interplay between syntax and semantics. Logic and Games Johan van Benthem Link These lecture notes provide an informal introduction to the connections between logic and game theory, a topic with deep roots in model theory. The author's engaging style makes this a very accessible text that connects to modern research in logic and computer science.

Number Theory

Title Author(s) URL Description

It provides historical context and intuition behind the key results, and it is aimed at inspiring the next generation of number theorists.

Topology

Title Author(s) URL Description
Topology: A Categorical Approach Tai-Danae Bradley, Tyler Bryson, and John Terilla Link This is a textbook for an introductory course in topology from a modern, categorical perspective. It is written in an informal and engaging style and is a great resource for anyone who wants to learn about the subject from a contemporary viewpoint.

It is written in an informal and accessible style and provides a great introduction to the history and ideas of modern topology.
